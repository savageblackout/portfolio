  • OURrestroom

    OURrestroom is a MEAN-stack application that is part of a movement to educate business owners about the benefits of providing gender non-specific single-stall restrooms. Patrons can anonymously submit the name and address of a business that has single-stall restrooms that are gendered, and we will do the rest! The business will receive a postcard with facts about the benefits of creating a space for all of their patrons that is comfortable and easy to use.
